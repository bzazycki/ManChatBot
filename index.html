About the Hive

The Hive is an admin portal designed for organizations to configure and manage their AI-powered chatbot solutions. Tailored for assisting underserved populations, such as homeless individuals, this platform allows seamless control over themes, notifications, and system configurations. By streamlining chatbot management, we aim to enhance communication and deliver impactful assistance.
